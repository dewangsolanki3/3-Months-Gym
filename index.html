Weight Training

Weight training is a common type of strength training for developing the strength and size of skeletal muscles. It works for both whether you are seeking to loose or gain weight. It promotes fat-free body mass and lean muscle mass that we all work so hard for decreases with age. If we don't add strength training to our routine then it will turn into fat.

Cardio

A Cardio a day makes your heart strong & increases your lung capacity and also reduces the risk of heart attack, high cholesterol, high blood pressure, diabetes, and some forms of cancer.

Passive Workout

Range of motion exercises are performed to help maintain flexibility and mobility at the joint performed. In individuals who cannot perform active exercises like weight training, passive exercises can help reduce stiffness of a joint and keep it from freezing from a lack of movement.

Steam / Massage

We provide you with seperate steam rooms for men and women. Sitting in a steam room might significantly improve your cardiovascular health and improved circulation, especially in extremities. Improved circulation can lead to lowered blood pressure and a healthier heart.
